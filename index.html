What is News Room

News Room is for the non-casual news reader. People who have a specific or vocational need for the news. Think stock brokers who might be following a stock. Or an environment who wants to stay updated on sea levels. Examples are endless.

News Room would provide a service by which users can subscribe to the subject matter of their choosing, from the simple to the complex.
